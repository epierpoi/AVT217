Dream Perfect Regime Poster

This poster is inspired by the music and artistic style of the Korean music and video group, Dream Perfect Regime. The colorful and lively aesthetic of their music and unique vision is shown through vibrant color schemes, collage of images from their music videos, and variety of differently placed text, featuring names of their songs from their album, "Is Anybody Out There?"

Program: Adobe InDesign
