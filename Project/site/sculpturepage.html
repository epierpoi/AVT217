Kusama Interior Sculpture

This sculpture was created in 3D Design with Professor Davis. The instructions were to make a sculpture that creates an interior space inspired by a piece of art or artist's style. I chose the work of Yayoi Kusama and made a mysterious forest of eyes, flowers, and dots, reminiscent of her work, inside a tissue box.

Materials: Clay, acrylic paint, tissue box, and fake leaves
