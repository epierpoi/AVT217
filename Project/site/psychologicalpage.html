Psychological Portrait

This drawing encompasses the fear of "growing up" through a timeline of myself from a baby until what I imagine myself to look like in the future. The faces physically connect and morph into one another to represent how these people are still me, shaped who I am today, and will be in the future. In the clouds are representations of things I found important in each of these stages of my life.

Media: Graphite drawing pencils
